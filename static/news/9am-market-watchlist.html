Smart money watchlist before the market opens

Why it matters: the first hour often decides whether the day is trend, trap, or chop.

This morning brief is designed for quick reading before the market settles. Instead of chasing every headline, Vishleshak Market Brief focuses on a few cues that can shape sentiment in the first trading hour.

01. Index moodCheck whether Sensex and Nifty open near support, resistance, or in a gap zone. Avoid reacting before the first 30-45 minutes confirm direction.
02. Sector rotationWatch whether money moves into banks, energy, auto, IT, FMCG, or pharma. Sector strength often tells a cleaner story than the headline index.
03. News triggerTrack policy comments, global cues, earnings, crude, rupee, and large-cap updates. One strong trigger can decide where attention goes today.

What it means for you:

Use the morning brief as a watchlist, not a trade call. It helps you know what to observe before making decisions. Educational only, not investment advice.
