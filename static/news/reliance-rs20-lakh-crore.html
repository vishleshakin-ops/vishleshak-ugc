Reliance crosses Rs.20 lakh crore market cap milestone

Why it matters: India's most valuable company just made history again.

Reliance Industries became the first Indian company to cross the Rs.20 lakh crore market capitalisation mark today, as its stock rallied 2.8% to close at Rs.2,945.

The surge was driven by a 3% overnight jump in global oil prices combined with strong refining margin guidance from the company's management. Energy stocks have quietly led the current market rally while investor attention stayed on IT.

What it means for you:

For investors, the milestone signals continued strength in large-cap energy names. Analysts suggest the refining and retail segments remain the key growth drivers heading into the next quarter, with several brokerages raising their price targets.

The broader market also closed higher, with the Sensex up 1.5% and the Nifty reclaiming the 23,400 level, supported by banking and energy sector gains.
